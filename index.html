Welcome to my website

Hey! I’m Alicia. I design bold, experimental visuals inspired by Fashion, movement, and gaming. This portfolio is where I drop school projects, creative experiments, and the stuff I’m proud of.

Main focuses right now

  • Animation & motion-based design
  • Branding & visual identity
  • Packaging & editorial layout

I’m especially interested in how design can communicate emotion through colour, typography, and movement. I enjoy experimenting with layouts and pushing concepts beyond the brief to create work that feels expressive but still intentional.

How I approach design

My process usually starts with research and visual exploration before moving into sketches and digital experimentation. I like refining ideas through feedback and iteration, focusing on clarity, balance, and strong visual storytelling in the final outcome.

A bit about me

I’m a graphic design student majoring in corporate design at George Brown College who loves bold and calming colours, expressive layouts, and anything inspired by fashion, movement, or gaming. My work mixes clean structure with playful energy — basically, organized chaos but make it pretty.
