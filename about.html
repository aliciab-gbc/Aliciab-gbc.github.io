About me

Some more information

I’m currently studying graphic design at George Brown College, where I’ve been developing my skills through branding, poster design, packaging, and digital projects. I enjoy experimenting with different styles while still focusing on clarity, structure, and strong visual storytelling.

Design i'm currently into

  • Art Nouveau
  • Surrealism
  • Punk

My design process usually starts with research and visual exploration, then moves into sketching and digital experimentation. I value iteration and feedback, and I’m always looking for ways to push an idea further while keeping the final outcome intentional and effective.

Inspiration & Direction

Outside of design, I’m inspired by fashion, dance, music, and games, which often influence the way I approach movement, rhythm, and composition in my work. I’m interested in continuing to grow my skills and exploring creative opportunities in branding, motion, and digital design.
